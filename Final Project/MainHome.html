A BIT MORE ABOUT US

Fifi's Store originate from the hilly farming areas that form the border regions of England with both Scotland and Whales, These rugged landscape saw the necessity for an all purpose stock and herding dog from which the border collie was born. Intelligence and tenacity, speed and agility and the ability to work tirelessy from morning until night were the valued assets in the dogs and the form the basic characteristics of the breed.

WHAT WE DO

At Fifi's Store, We breed working Border Collies with care and a special attention given to the genetic and heriditary traits.We have the improvement of this unique breed at heart and try to give birth to the most amazing working line ever.

WHY WE DO

Border Collies are the dogs we breed, the dogs we love and the dogs we deveote our life to. They never talk about themselves but listen to you while you talk about yourself, and keep up an appearance of being interested in the conversation.

HOW WE DO

Only our dogs are bred and, helped by vets and professionals, we handpicked their partners from other successful and famous bloodlines in order to equip the litters with a wonderful genetic variation.
